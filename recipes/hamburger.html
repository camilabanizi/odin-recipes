Hamburger with fries

Description

The best hamburger and fries to relax after a really long day of work. This burger is packed with all of the best ingredients, you are gonna be left asking for more!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Beef
  • Potatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Sauces

Steps

  1. Make five patties.
  2. Cut the potatoes and fry them.
  3. Grill the patties.
  4. Add everything (don't forget the cheese, lettuce, and sauces!) to the bread.
